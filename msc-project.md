# Master's Project

## Title
Ginger–Honey Derived Carbon Quantum Dots

## Abstract
This project investigates the synthesis of carbon quantum dots derived from ginger and honey using a green hydrothermal process. The photoluminescence properties of the nanoparticles were characterized and potential sensing applications were explored.

## Techniques
- Hydrothermal synthesis
- Photoluminescence spectroscopy
- Nanomaterial characterization
